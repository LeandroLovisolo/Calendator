Calendator The printable calendar generator

How to use it

  1. Click on a date to mark it
  2. Write annotations at the right
  3. Print it

About Calendator

Calendator lets you quickly print calendars that you can use to mark important dates and stick it on your wall, draft estimates during a meeting, or simply doodle your schedule.

Built by Leandro Lovisolo.
